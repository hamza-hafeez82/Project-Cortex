# Responsible Use Policy

Project Cortex is a research project focused on safe, interpretable, 
and biologically grounded Artificial General Intelligence. 
Although the repository is licensed under the Apache License 2.0, 
the following restrictions apply to all uses of the material:

## Prohibited Uses
You may NOT use this repository or any derivative work for:
- Development or deployment of autonomous weapons
- Surveillance systems that violate privacy or human rights
- Harmful biological modeling or biosecurity threat analysis
- Unsupervised AGI agents with unrestricted autonomy
- Military targeting or decision making systems
- Any application intended to cause harm, coercion, or manipulation

## Required Principles
Users must:
- Commit to responsible and ethical AI development
- Maintain transparency, auditability, and oversight
- Implement safeguards against misuse or unintended behavior
- Follow international human rights and safety standards

## Disclaimer
This repository provides conceptual research materials,
not production-grade AGI systems. The authors assume no responsibility 
for any misuse, modification, or deployment of derived works.

By accessing or using this repository, you agree to comply with this policy.
